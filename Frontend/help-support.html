Rules & Regulations

Please familiarize yourself with our community guidelines and policies

Respectful Behavior

All users must maintain respectful and professional communication. Harassment, bullying, or discrimination of any kind will not be tolerated.

Academic Integrity

Plagiarism and academic dishonesty are strictly prohibited. All submitted work must be your own, with proper attribution when using external sources.

Privacy & Security

Protect your account credentials and respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information without consent.

Content Guidelines

All shared content must be appropriate and relevant to learning. Inappropriate, offensive, or spam content will be removed.

Collaboration Policy

Collaboration is encouraged for learning, but individual assignments must be completed independently unless otherwise specified.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about BrillX

How do I reset my password?
You can reset your password by clicking on "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.
Can I download course materials for offline use?
Yes, most course materials can be downloaded for offline use. Look for the download icon next to each resource. Some materials may have restrictions based on the instructor's preferences.
How do I contact my instructor?
You can contact your instructor through the messaging system within each course. Go to the course page and look for the "Instructor" or "Messages" section. Most instructors respond within 24-48 hours.
What should I do if I'm having technical issues?
First, try refreshing the page and clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, check our status page for any ongoing system issues. For persistent problems, contact our technical support team through this help form.
How do I get a certificate for a completed course?
Certificates are automatically generated when you complete all course requirements, including assignments, tests, and achieving a passing grade. You can download your certificate from the "My Certificates" section in your dashboard.
