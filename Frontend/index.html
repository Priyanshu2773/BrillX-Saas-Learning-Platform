Frequently Asked Questions

BrillX is an AI-powered learning platform that revolutionizes education through personalized learning experiences. Our platform uses advanced algorithms to adapt learning materials to each user's preferences, skills, and pace, while also featuring intelligent knowledge extraction from PDFs to transform static documents into interactive learning content.

BrillX uses sophisticated AI algorithms to analyze your learning patterns, preferences, and performance. The system adapts content difficulty, learning pace, and material presentation based on your individual needs, ensuring optimal knowledge retention and engagement throughout your learning journey.

Our intelligent knowledge extraction technology transforms standard PDF documents into interactive learning materials. The system identifies key concepts, creates structured learning paths, generates quizzes, and extracts relevant examples from your PDFs, making static content dynamic and engaging for effective learning.

BrillX covers a wide range of subjects with a focus on technology and professional skills. Our current categories include Web Development, App Development, Data Analysis, and Machine Learning, with more subjects being added regularly through our AI-powered content generation and extraction capabilities.

Getting started with BrillX is simple! Just click the Sign Up button to create your account. Our AI will guide you through an initial assessment to understand your learning preferences and goals, then create a personalized learning path tailored specifically to your needs. You can also upload your own PDFs to transform them into interactive learning materials.
